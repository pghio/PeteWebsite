## Selected Research & Writing

*Empirical, methodology-first studies of LLM behavior — the core of why this role fits.*

- **"We Asked 8 LLMs to Run a Family's Life"** — Benchmarked 8 models (Claude
  Haiku/Sonnet/Opus, GPT-4o-mini/4.1/5.4, Gemini Flash, DeepSeek V3) across ~2,800
  production API calls. Characterized **behavioral defaults and failure modes**: a
  45% tool-call hallucination rate on one model vs. correct conversation-recognition
  on the Claude family, a 67% ceiling on ambiguous input across all models, and a
  Claude-specific JSON-in-markdown behavior. Open-source harness.
- **"Do LLMs Actually Cite Your Startup?"** — 90-day empirical study with full
  methodology, custom attribution instrumentation, and a replication guide.
- **"The SEO Engine That Grades Its Own Work"** — Built an **LLM-as-judge** grading
  layer (quality / spam-risk / PII scoring) gating publication — eval design and
  calibration in production.
- **Intent-taxonomy work (in progress)** — Classifying real user requests into a
  ranked behavioral map, held to a strict "publish only at N≥300" data bar.
